Who We Are

CSE Ambassadors seek to bridge the digital divide. Using our professional skills in Computer Science and Engineering, we work to address our community's technology needs. We are highly motivated students who take responsibility for outreach, promotion of diversity and equality, and education. We create solutions for our community's computing problems.
